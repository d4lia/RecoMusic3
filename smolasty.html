Smolasty

Smolasty , real name Norbert Smoliński (born on August 27, 1995 in Warsaw), is a Polish singer, music producer and rapper whose main genres of music are on the border of R&B , pop and rap.

He has collaborated with artists such as Kaz Balagane, Otsochodzi, Białas, MIYO, Malik Montana, ReTo, Chada, PlanBe, Young Multi, Mr Poland and Frosti Regge.

As a teenager, he was interested in rap and funk music and he founded a funk music band, in which he played, among others on the guitar. He attended school in Warsaw Bródno. In later years, he began to be inspired by the music of the 60s and 70s , as well as the work of rap artists such as Chris Brown and Omarion.

He was nominated for “debut album of the year” at the Frederyki 2018 awards, the main awards of the Polish music community. In 2019, he was nominated in the “music artist” category at the Joy Poland awards and also was nominated in the “pop” category for his music video “Raj” at the PL Music Video Awards. In February of 2019, Fake Love was certificated a gold record for having 15,000+ sales in Poland.
